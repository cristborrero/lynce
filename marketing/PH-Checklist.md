# ProductHunt Launch Checklist: LYNCE

## Pre-Launch (1 Week Before)
- [ ] Prepare high-quality visual assets (1270x760 images).
- [ ] Create a "first comment" explaining the *why* behind LYNCE.
- [ ] Record a 1-minute video demo of a scan in action.
- [ ] Reach out to 5-10 "Security Hunters" on PH for support.

## Launch Day (The Big Day)
- [ ] Launch at 12:01 AM PST.
- [ ] Share on Twitter, LinkedIn, and Reddit (r/wordpress, r/saas).
- [ ] Respond to EVERY comment on PH within 15 minutes.
- [ ] Update landing page with "We are live on ProductHunt" banner.

## Post-Launch (The Day After)
- [ ] Announce "PH Special" discount for Pro plan (e.g. 50% off for 6 months).
- [ ] Send email to existing users about the PH launch.
- [ ] Capture all feedback and add to the "Changelog" backlog.
